~ About ~

What makes PRT unique?

At PRT, there is great determination to help each learner with their schoolwork, especially to excel in their subjects. Each learner gets their own private time with Teacher Tiensie. Parents receive progress reports to see that Teacher Tiensie improves their skills each week. The environment offered at PRT, is motivation and a strive for success!

* Vision *

The vision is to create a fundamental base and understanding of education in English, Maths, and Afrikaans, therefore aiming to witness a progression in marks.

* Mission *

The end goal is to achieve success in the learner's education and that the learners are confident to complete their schoolwork, therefore wanting learners to proceed to the next grade.

Success Stories

Petrus

Petrus was in Teacher Tiensie's Grade 2 class when she taught full-time at Oos Skool, now known as Millenium. After Petrus finished school, he stepped on a landmine during his time in the Army and lost both his eyes. Regardless of this, Petrus and Teacher Tiensie have been in contact to this day. The significance of their story is the reciprication of their relationship: Teacher Tiensie taught Petrus how to read and write, and now Petrus has taught Teacher Tiensie how to use braille on a phone.

Adri

Adri was in Teacher Tiensie's class in Grade 1 until Grade 3 in Westrand School for Physically Disabled Children. Afterwards, she was transferred to general school, Highschool, and then finished University with multiple Degrees in Teaching. Now, Adri has begun her career as a teacher, following in the footsteps of Teacher Tiensie.
